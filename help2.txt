Grimoire - Chapter 2: Useful Spells
Spells:

 .test - meant to test if the book is working

 .nap <time> - sleeps for a number of seconds

 .solo <member> <role> - voice mutes all members of the role except for the single member

 .unsolo <role> - voice unmutes all members of the role

 .silence <minutes> <users> - silences the listed users for the listed amount of time. 

 .help - used to show commands for a certain chapter

 .chapter <1,2,3> - change to a different chapter
