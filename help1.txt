Grimoire - Chapter 1: Basic Spells
Spells:

.echo - have grim repeat what you say, and magically remove your own voice

 .insult <word> - add an insult to grim

.help - shows the spells for a certain chapter

.chapter <1,2,3> - chooses a chapter to go to
